Starry Nine: A rookie investor's reference

Introduction

The covid-19 pandemic affected globally, stock market is not an exception. Our group is interested to study its impact on the Malaysia Stock Market between January 2020 to May 2021. And this dashboard will be able to give you and insight into the market performance during this period.


Objectives

  • To present the general overview of the Malaysia stock market
  • To allow users to compare stocks or view details of each stock over time
  • To present our analysis to help amateur investors like us to make well-informed decisions

Research Questions

  1. How did the Bursa Malaysia stock market perform during the covid-19 pandemic?
  2. Which sectors are the most affected by the pandemic?
